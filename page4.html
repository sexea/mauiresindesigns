Black Mango

$1100

Resin: Black

Dimensions: 83.5”L x 28.25”W

Wood: Mango

This Mango slab was rescued from a 100 year old decaying Mango tree in Lahaina. The tree was being removed to prevent further damage, so it was salvaged and repurposed for this table. I was able to repair the crevice with an inlay of resin bowties to make it stable and add further design detail.
