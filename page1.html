Melia River

$1900

Resin: Blue Pearl

Dimensions: 55"Lx16"W

Wood: Melia

This Melia wood slab originates from a tree that was originally located near the Hui No'eua visual Arts Center on the Historic Kaluanoi Estate in Makawao. The tree had been removed from the Estate to make room for and to allow for an older Monkey Pod tree to be able to continue to grow and spread out without being impeded. At first glance we knew that it would be an interesting looking piece if we filled it with resin to bring out its depth and curves.

We found this slab in the mud alongside another similar piece in Haiku, Maui (which is also from the same tree as the piece we used in the Red Pearl table).
